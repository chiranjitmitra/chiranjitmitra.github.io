About

Dr. Chiranjit Mitra was born on 2nd December, 1990 to Mr. Prabir Kumar Mitra & Mrs. Gayatri Mitra in the resplendent city of Jamshedpur (Tatanagar) in India. He pursued his matriculation & intermediate studies at Narbheram Hansraj English School (N.H.E.S.), Jamshedpur, India.

He was offered the prestigious KVPY (Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana) fellowship in 2009 along with an opportunity of joining one of the Indian Institutes of Science Education and Research. Subsequently, he moved to Kolkata, India where he pursued a 5 year B.S. - M.S. Dual Degree in Physical Sciences at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Kolkata, India.

Thereafter, Dr. Mitra moved to Germany where he pursued a Ph.D. in Physics (Specialization: Theoretical Physics) at the Department of Physics, Humboldt University (HU) of Berlin, Germany while he worked as a doctoral researcher in the BMBF Young Investigators Group CoSy-CC2 at the Potsdam Institute for Climate Impact Research (PIK), Germany (Institute of the Leibniz Association).

In early 2018, Dr. Mitra started with his present appointment as a postdoctoral fellow at the Department of Physics and Astronomy, Northwestern University (NU), USA.
